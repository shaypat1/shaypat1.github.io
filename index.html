About Us

Traders at CMU is a student organization committed to uncovering the black box of quantitative finance.

The trading industry is a zero-sum game. To win, someone must lose. This idea has shaped the industry into a competitive and secretive place, to the point where passionate minds are struggling to learn.

Traders at CMU aims to teach and to trade; our goal is to provide an environment for students to pursue their passion for trading, hone their skills, and apply their ideas in different markets. We believe in learning by doing—through structured education sessions, different trading teams, and competitions around campus, our traders gain firsthand experience in algorithmic trading, market structure and dynamics, and quantitative analysis.

Ultimately, our mission is to equip every member with the tools, confidence, and network to excel in the world of trading and beyond.

Equities Team (Advised by Armon Singh)

The Equities Team at CMU focuses on identifying and trading opportunities in the stock market, leveraging cutting-edge quantitative methods and rigorous analysis. Our team of students researches a wide array of equities from diverse sectors, continuously refining a systematic approach to trading. Our process begins with the development of robust signals based on fundamental, technical, and alternative data sources. These signals are then subjected to thorough backtesting against historical market conditions to ensure their reliability and effectiveness before any real-world application.

Risk management is central to our workflow. We employ sophisticated position-sizing techniques and adopt a disciplined framework to evaluate drawdowns and market volatility. Once strategies are validated, our team implements them through Interactive Brokers, carefully monitoring live performance. By combining academic theory with practical experience, team members gain firsthand knowledge of portfolio construction, execution mechanics, and the intricacies of the global financial markets. Beyond trading, we also emphasize the importance of continuous learning through regular seminars, research presentations, and peer-to-peer mentorship. This collaborative environment helps members stay at the forefront of developments in quantitative finance and ensures that the Equities Team remains highly adaptive and innovative.

Sports Markets Team (Advised by Cheney Price)

The Sports Market Team at CMU specializes in analyzing and participating in various sports-based exchange platforms. Our team focuses on leveraging statistical methods and advanced modeling techniques to project potential outcomes in a wide range of sports events. We gather data from numerous reliable sources, harnessing insights from both macro-level trends and granular game-level details. These insights are used to develop systematic strategies, which are rigorously backtested on historical datasets to ensure robust performance across different market conditions.

The team executes trades on platforms like Novig and Sporttrade, where real-time pricing and liquidity require a keen understanding of market dynamics. Each member gains hands-on experience in risk and reward assessment, position sizing, and performance evaluation. Collaboration is key: we hold regular strategy roundtables to discuss emerging sports data, adjust our models, and refine our decision-making processes. Through this combination of theoretical knowledge and practical market exposure, members acquire a deeper appreciation of how quantitative analytics can guide successful trading in sports markets. Our mission is to foster an environment where intellectual curiosity and methodical research converge to drive innovation and consistent results.

Crypto Team (Partnered with Carnegie Mellon Blockchain)

The Crypto Team at CMU delves into the rapidly evolving world of digital assets, exploring innovative trading strategies and blockchain-based technologies. Partnered with Carnegie Mellon Blockchain, the team benefits from a vibrant network of industry experts, academic researchers, and fellow enthusiasts who are shaping the future of decentralized finance. Our approach encompasses analyzing both on-chain and off-chain data to uncover market inefficiencies and trends that can lead to profitable strategies.

We emphasize a structured research process: team members conceptualize trading signals rooted in blockchain analytics, liquidity assessments, and market psychology. These signals undergo rigorous backtesting across diverse crypto market conditions, ensuring resilience and adaptability. Once strategies are optimized, they are deployed on select cryptocurrency exchanges, with real-time monitoring and ongoing performance evaluations. A core focus is risk management, as cryptocurrency markets can be highly volatile; we employ robust hedging techniques and disciplined stop-loss mechanisms. Education and collaboration are central to our culture—regular workshops cover topics such as decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols, smart contract security, and emerging blockchain applications. By blending academic insights, real-world trading experience, and the supportive ecosystem provided by Carnegie Mellon Blockchain, our Crypto Team stands at the forefront of digital asset innovation.
